# This is the homepage of TrackaList Welcome to TrackaList!

Planner Pain?

Trackalist is a simple web project that lets students like you track their assignments through a simple list!

What's so good about Trackalist?

Trackalist being a simple web application is the best part of it!

Simplicity is important to avoid that overwhelming sense of a planner. The purpose of a planner is to help you with your day-to-day life, not to cause more stress!


Trackalist features include:

Creating an assigment/task

Creating a simple list of these tasks that are very well organized

A checklist to mark when you've finished a task

A simple timer

Cute themes and colors

And more!
