Trackalist offers a free app template for you to personalize to your own needs.

Simplicity is important to avoid that overwhelming sense of planning out your day. The purpose of a planner is to help you with your day-to-day life, not to cause more stress!


Trackalist features include:

  • Creating an assignment/task
  • Creating a simple list of these tasks that are very well organized
  • A checklist to mark when you've finished a task
  • A simple timer
  • Cute themes and colors
  • And more!
